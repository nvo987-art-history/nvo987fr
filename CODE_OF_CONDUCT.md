# Code of Conduct

## Purpose

The NVO987 project aims to maintain a respectful and professional environment for collaboration related to research, culture, and digital infrastructure.

## Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:

- communicate respectfully
- contribute constructively
- respect intellectual work and academic standards
- support an open and collaborative research environment

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not acceptable:

- harassment or discrimination
- abusive language
- personal attacks
- deliberate disruption of the project

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all contributions and interactions related to the NVO987 website and repository.

Project:

NVO987 – Culture Visuelle Moderne et Contemporaine  
https://nvo987.fr
